# Form4 Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Form4
**Tagline:** Insider Intelligence, Decoded
**URL:** form4.app

## Logo

The Form4 logo consists of two elements:
- **Icon Mark:** A bold blue "4" with an upward arrow integrated into the diagonal stroke, on a dark rounded square
- **Wordmark:** "Form" in white + "4" in Signal Blue

The icon mark can be used standalone (favicon, app icon, social profile, video watermark).
The wordmark is used alongside the icon for headers, banners, and marketing materials.

## Typography

- **Body/UI:** Inter (weights: 400, 500, 600, 700, 800)
- **Data/Numbers:** JetBrains Mono (monospace)
- **Headlines:** Inter Black (900) for marketing/video content

## Voice & Tone

- **Authoritative but accessible.** "Here's what the data says" not "You should buy this"
- **Data-driven.** Lead with numbers, back with research
- **No hedging.** Direct, confident, concise
- **Never financial advice.** Disclaimer in descriptions, never in content itself

## Social Media

### Profile Picture
The icon mark (blue arrow-4) on dark background. No text.

### Bio Template
"Insider Intelligence, Decoded. Real-time SEC Form 4 alerts + AI signal grading. 7-day free trial ↓ form4.app"

### Video Content
- Watermark: Small icon mark in top-right corner
- Opening frame: Form4 logo + "Top Insider Trades" (date overlaid in CapCut)
- Color coding: Green = buy, Red = sell, Amber = alert (never deviate)
- Closing frame: Form4 logo + tagline + "form4.app" + "7-day free trial"
